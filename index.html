About us

Gardner neighborhood is located in San Jose, California, bounded by Interstate 280 to the North, Guadalupe River to the East, Bird Avenue to the West, and Caltrain tracks to the South. Gardner Neighborhood Association is a group of neighbors working together to better the community. Our meeting minutes and bylaws can be found here.

Our current board members are:

  • President Armando Ricardez
  • Vice President Robert Jones
  • Secretary Gabriela Banks
  • Treasurer Marc Douat
  • Member at large Kevin L. Christman
  • Member at large Patricia Palomares-Mason
